## Negative Keywords — Filter OUT

| Term to Suppress | Why | Use Instead |
|---|---|---|
| AI news | 10,000 results, mostly consumer AI | "AI FP&A news" |
| ChatGPT [feature] | Consumer product — not enterprise focus | "reasoning models enterprise" |
| AI will replace jobs | Opinion content, clickbait | "AI skill premium" or "AI workforce transition" |
| best AI tools | Product listicles — vendor marketing | "AI financial modeling tools benchmark" |
| AI prompt tips | Consumer tutorial content | "Prompt engineering enterprise" |
| AI hype | Meta-commentary — second-order coverage | Specific capability claims with source attribution |
| blockchain AI | Speculation — almost always low quality | Unless specific verified enterprise deployment |
| AGI [general] | Philosophical debate — minimal operational relevance | "AI reasoning benchmark" |
| AI startup funding [generic] | Generic rounds without domain relevance | "AI FP&A startup funding" |
